About Me

I’m Ean Endrew Jade Sinining, a third-year college student at the University of Mindanao with a growing passion for web development and eCommerce. I’ve gained hands-on experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS, and have served as the frontend developer for a major school project, building key system features from the ground up. While design doesn’t come naturally to me, I’m consistently working to improve my skills and create user-friendly interfaces. As a Freelance Shopify Store Assistant, I help clients set up their online stores, customize themes, manage product listings, and ensure mobile responsiveness.

I’m currently focused on becoming a more well-rounded developer by improving both my frontend and backend skills. I’m also exploring Vue.js and Laravel to deepen my understanding of modern web frameworks. Beyond the web, I’ve contributed to Arduino-based projects and developed a payroll system using Django as part of a team. I value continuous learning, transparency, and adaptability, and I’m driven by a strong desire to grow professionally and contribute to real-world tech solutions that make a difference.
